About Dr. Parul Jadhav

Educator. Researcher. Innovator.

Dr. Parul Jadhav

Head of Department, MIT-WPU

Dr. Jadhav is a leading academician with over 20 years of experience in research and education. She specializes in Artificial Intelligence, Smart Systems, and Data-Driven Innovation. Her work spans across academia, student mentoring, and institutional leadership.

Career Timeline

  • 2023–Present: HoD, MIT-WPU
  • 2017–2023: Associate Professor, MIT-WPU
  • 2012–2017: PhD in Artificial Intelligence
  • 2007–2012: Assistant Professor, VIT
