About Dr. Parul Jadhav

Educator. Researcher. Innovator.

Dr. Parul Jadhav is an Associate Professor in the School of Electronics and Communication at MIT WPU. She has over 29 years of experience in teaching, learning, research, and administration across diverse domains and technologies. Her areas of interest are in signal processing, video analytics, and Artificial Intelligence.
